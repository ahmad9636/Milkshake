Mango Milkshake Delight!

A refreshing and creamy treat for any time of the day.

Indulge in the luscious taste of summer with this easy-to-make mango milkshake recipe. Perfect for a hot day or as a delightful dessert!

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1.5 cups cold milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 2-3 tablespoons sugar or honey (adjust to taste)
  • 4-5 ice cubes (optional, for extra chill)
  • A pinch of cardamom powder (optional, for flavor)
  • Fresh mint leaves or a slice of mango for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the chopped mangoes, cold milk, and sugar/honey.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy. If you prefer a thinner consistency, add a little more milk.
  3. Add the ice cubes (if using) and blend again until crushed and incorporated.
  4. If using, add a pinch of cardamom powder and give it a final quick blend.
  5. Pour the mango milkshake into serving glasses.
  6. Garnish with fresh mint leaves or a slice of mango, if desired.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing homemade mango milkshake!

Tips for the Best Mango Milkshake

  • Use ripe mangoes: The sweeter and riper the mangoes, the better your milkshake will taste.
  • Chill your ingredients: Using cold milk and chilled mangoes will result in a more refreshing milkshake.
  • Adjust sweetness: Taste the milkshake before serving and add more sugar or honey if needed.
  • Experiment with flavors: A tiny bit of vanilla extract or a dash of saffron can also enhance the taste.
